Cybersecurity for Kids

Cybersecurity for Kids introduces young learners to the basics of staying safe online. Through fun, interactive lessons, kids will learn how the internet works, how to protect their personal information, and how to use technology responsibly and securely.

What you will learn:

Understanding online safety and digital citizenship
How to create strong passwords and recognize scams
Identifying safe and unsafe websites and apps
Protecting devices from viruses and malware
Online kindness, privacy, and responsible communication
Basic coding projects about cybersecurity concepts

Cybersecurity Modules

Introduction to Online Safety

Learn about the importance of staying safe online. Kids will explore how the internet works, why privacy matters, and how to make smart choices when sharing information online.

Passwords & Personal Security

Discover how to create strong passwords and protect your accounts. Students will play password-strength games and learn how hackers try to trick people into revealing information.

Understanding Malware & Scams

Kids will learn about computer viruses, phishing, and online scams in a simple, visual way. They’ll discover how to spot suspicious links and messages before clicking them.

Coding for Cyber Safety

Through fun coding exercises, kids will create mini projects that simulate cybersecurity scenarios—like building a password checker or detecting fake websites.

Responsible Digital Citizenship

Students will explore topics like online kindness, privacy, and how to respond to cyberbullying. They’ll learn that being safe online also means being respectful and kind.

Cyber Challenges & Projects

Apply everything learned through fun games and challenges. Kids will earn badges as they complete activities that test their cybersecurity knowledge and skills.
