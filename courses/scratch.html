Scratch Programming for Kids

Scratch Programming introduces kids to the world of coding through fun, colorful, and interactive projects. Learners will create animations, games, and stories while building logical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

What you will learn:

Understanding coding concepts using blocks
Creating fun games and stories with characters
Learning loops, conditions, and events through play
Building animations and interactive projects
Collaborating and sharing projects on Scratch
Developing creative problem-solving skills

Scratch Programming Modules

Introduction to Scratch

Get started with Scratch! Learn how to navigate the interface, use coding blocks, and bring characters (sprites) to life through movement and color.

Game Design Basics

Create exciting games by learning how to use loops, events, and scoring systems. Build your own mini-games like mazes and clicker challenges!

Animations & Stories

Use coding to tell your own stories! Kids will animate characters, add dialogue, and create fun scenes with sound effects and motion.

Logic & Control

Learn the basics of coding logic — loops, conditions, and sequences — all through visual block coding in Scratch’s friendly environment.

Creative Projects

Kids will design their own games and animations from scratch, applying creativity and problem-solving to make unique, interactive creations.

Showcase & Sharing

Students will polish their projects, present them to peers, and learn how to share their creations safely on the Scratch community platform.
