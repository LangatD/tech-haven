Why Learn Coding

Develops Problem-Solving Skills

Coding teaches children how to break down complex problems into smaller, manageable pieces. They learn to think logically and creatively, skills that help them excel in all areas of life.

Improves Math and Analytical Skills

Coding makes math fun and practical! As kids write code, they naturally use logic, patterns, and calculations, strengthening their mathematical thinking in an engaging way.

Prepares for Future Careers

No matter which field your child pursues; medicine, business, arts, or technology; coding skills will give them a competitive advantage in tomorrow's digital world.

Enhances Creativity and Expression

Coding is digital creativity! Kids can design games, build interactive stories, create animations, and bring their wildest imaginations to life through code.

Frequently asked questions

My child is a beginner is previous coding experience required?

Not at all! Our courses are designed with beginners in mind. We start with the basics and guide each child step by step through fun, interactive lessons. No prior coding experience is required, just curiosity and a willingness to learn!

What kind of device does my child need to start learning?

Your child can learn using any computer or laptop with a stable internet connection. We recommend a device with a keyboard and a modern web browser like Chrome or Edge for the best experience. Tablets can work for some activities, but a computer or laptop makes it easier to code comfortably and follow along with lessons.

How will I stay updated on my child’s progress?

You’ll receive regular updates on your child’s progress through our parent dashboard and email reports. You’ll be able to see what they’re learning, the projects they’ve completed, and their overall improvement. Our instructors also share feedback and suggestions to help you stay involved in your child’s learning journey.

What is the recommended age for kids to start learning coding?

We recommend starting as early as 7 years old, though kids of all ages can begin learning to code! Our programs are designed to match different age groups and skill levels, younger learners start with fun, visual programming, while older kids progress to text-based coding and real projects.

How is my child privacy data protected?

Your child’s privacy and data are fully protected. We follow strict security standards and never share personal information with third parties. All learning takes place in a safe, monitored online environment.

How do I enroll my child to Tech haven?

You can enroll your child by booking a free trial class. After the trial, you can choose a course and complete the enrollment process online.

Can I reschedule a class for my child?

Yes, you can reschedule your child’s class up to 24 hours in advance. Simply contact our support team or use your parent dashboard to pick a new time that works for you.

What if my child misses a class?

If your child misses a class, we’ll arrange a make-up lesson during their next booked session. This ensures they stay on track without missing any important concepts.

Can I communicate with my child’s teacher?

Yes, absolutely! Parents can communicate directly with their child’s teacher through the parent dashboard or by messaging our support team. We encourage open communication to keep you informed about your child’s progress and learning experience
